Case Title: Breach of Contract for Non-Delivery of Goods

Summary:
The plaintiff entered into a contract with the defendant for the supply of 500 units of electronic components by June 2023. Despite repeated follow-ups, the defendant failed to deliver the goods. The agreement included clear terms of delivery and penalties for delays. The plaintiff seeks compensation for the loss incurred due to breach of contract and lost business.

Legal Focus:
- Contract Law
- Commercial Agreement Enforcement
- Breach & Remedies

Requested Outcome:
- Refund of advance payment
- Compensation for business losses due to delay

Notes:
No subjective factors or human discretion (e.g., mental health, morality) are involved. The contract terms and timelines are clearly defined.
